Visit

The family of Dr. Harmon will be hosting memorial visitation hours at their residence on Tuesday May 31st from 7pm and 9pm and Wednesday June 1st from 7pm to 9pm. Address provided upon email request.

Donate

In lieu of flowers, Dr. Harmon's family requests contributions to be made in his honor to the The Dr. William Harmon Tribute Fund. Proceeds will go to the Division of Nephrology at Boston Children's Hospital for Kidney Transplant Clinical Research.
